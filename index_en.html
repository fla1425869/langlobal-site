About Us

Langlobal, founded in 2017, offers translation and proofreading services across a wide range of languages, reflecting the diverse linguistic skills of our team.

We are a team with diverse communication skills. Therefore, we offer translation and proofreading services in different language combinations, with our members being native speakers of Portuguese, Ukrainian, and Russian, and fluent in English and French.
